1. A business can grow without tracking anything.
False – Growth without tracking isn’t sustainable.

2. Follower count is the best way to measure success.
False – Followers are a vanity metric, not true success.

3. Customer opinions matter more than sales numbers.
False – Feedback and sales both matter.

4. A good decision can be made with no data.
False – Good decisions usually need some data.

5. Data is always fair.
False – Data can be biased.
